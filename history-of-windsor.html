Getting Started

Take a look at the video above: The Origins of the Windsors Explained. This short but informative 15 minute clip will provide you with details on the origins of the Royal Family. It is a fantastic starting point if you want to learn more about Windsor and its monarchy, as it has such a deep and rich history.

Interesting Facts

  • Windsor is home to Britain’s shortest street, Queen Charlotte Street, which is located in the High Street next to the Crooked House of Windsor.
  • …It’s also home to the only blue letterbox in the country! (The rest of them are red)
  • The Castle contains 450 clocks.
  • Windsor Castle caught fire in 1992!
  • The Castle has the oldest working kitchen in the country.
  • The Royal Family actually got their Family name from the Castle - it used to be Saxe-Coburg.

Sources used: (The London Pass®, 2024), (Wolton, 2018)
