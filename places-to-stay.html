Places Near The Castle

The first hotel to mention is The Castle Hotel (shown in the image to the left), which is situated right next to Windsor Castle, and is an absolutely luxurious place to stay.

Another one worth mentioning is The Sir Christopher Wren Hotel, as it’s also very close by to the Castle (just a 6 minute walk). A family friend stayed here before while visiting us, and thoroughly enjoyed her time here.

Other Locations

The De Vere Beaumont Estate, an absolutely stunning building, is just a short drive away from the castle. The many acres of land surrounding this place are amazing.

This one is an interesting one, but Legoland Windsor Resort has its own hotel! It is a little pricey, but definitely looks like loads of fun.

Trusted Travel Resources

Booking.com, Hotels.com, and Expedia are all fantastic booking sites which will provide you with the best offers on accommodation in Windsor. You can filter results to your liking, as my suggested hotels might not be suited to you - they’re just recommendations after all!
