About Me

Hi, welcome to my site, I hope you enjoy your stay. (But I hope you enjoy your stay in Windsor even more!)


My name is Chloe, as you may have seen before. Windsor is my home, so I’m hoping to share some useful information about this beautiful place in the hopes that you decide to visit one day!


A little bit about me: I’ve lived in Windsor for a lot of my life, but I’m currently a student in Manchester. Although the lifestyle in the city has been different, I’ll always love the comfort that Windsor gives me.


Please, take a look around, and if my tour guide doesn’t inspire you to visit, I hope you at least learn something worthwhile.

Throughout this guide you’ll find some of my favourite places to visit in and around Windsor, some delicious eateries, and some helpful details about accommodation and getting around.
