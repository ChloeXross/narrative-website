Overview

Here I’ve put together some resources that might help you if you decide to visit Windsor. I feel like this page is a nice place to collate and reiterate information that I’ve mentioned before (such as hotels), and provide some new info that may be of interest!

If you scroll to the end of the page, I’ve linked some fun little tours that you might enjoy and may help you if you’re still looking for inspiration on things to do.

Getting Around

Travelling to a new place can be intimidating, whether you live in the same country or not. I know that it always takes me some time to get used to the public transport in a different area, so I’ve provided some links to some trusted companies and other resources that might help you along your way.

Taxis

  • Windsor Cars - Myself and family members have all used and been satisfied with the service of these taxis!
  • Windsor Taxis - This service offers transport to and from the airport.
  • Royal Windsor Taxis - Another trusted taxi service.

Other Resources

  • Windsor has 2 train stations: Windsor & Eton Central, and Windsor & Eton Riverside.
  • Thames Valley Buses operates in the Windsor & Slough area.
  • Find more information on the Royal Borough of Windsor & Maidenhead public transport page.

Popular Tours

It can be hard to even know where to begin when visiting somewhere new, but Windsor has plenty of well curated tours to offer.

(© Credit to Martin Pettitt for this image of London's Duck Tours.)

  • Duck Boat Tours - This one sounds fun: and it is! You get taken on a tour through the centre of Windsor, and then (in the same vehicle) slip into the river and continue the tour along the Thames (they also do them in London if you fancy a day-trip).
  • Get Your Guide - Here you can find multiple Windsor based tours, and filter the duration, price, language and time as well!
